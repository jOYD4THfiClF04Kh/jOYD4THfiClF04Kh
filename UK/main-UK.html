Welcome.

On this site you’ll find:

  • Pictures and detailed information on mountain cabins in the Alps and other European regions.

  • Descriptions of beautiful walks to the breathtaking places where these cabins are situated.

  • Useful information to make the planning of your vacation in mountain regions a success.

  • Special mountain data with pictures and relevant links.

  • Information on equipment and clothing.

  • Current  weather information and many useful links to other sites.

This makes ‘Mountain cabins in Europe’ the perfect place to start your planning for high altitudes.

Although this site original is made in the Dutch language, and I’m doing my very best to translate it for you, sometimes you will be linked to my pages in the Dutch language because the pages you want to see are not translated yet.  I apologize for this inconvenience but rather than let you see nothing, I thought it would be better to link you at least to the Dutch pages.

Wiwannihütte   (2.463m - CH)
